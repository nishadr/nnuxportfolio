Fintech Evolution 2024

Redefining Digital Wealth Management

Role

Lead UX Designer

Timeline

6 Months

Platform

iOS & Web

Overview

The project aimed to transform a fragmented traditional banking experience into a unified, intelligent wealth management ecosystem. The primary challenge was balancing complex data visualization with an effortless user journey for both novice and experienced investors.

The Challenge

Users felt overwhelmed by legacy interfaces, resulting in a 40% drop-off rate during the portfolio rebalancing process.

The Objective

Create a "zero-friction" interface that leverages AI to provide actionable financial insights in under 3 seconds.

Figure 1.1: Early conceptual wireframes focusing on information hierarchy

The Process

  1. 1

    Deep Research & Discovery

    We interviewed 24 active investors across three demographics. Key insight: users didn't want more data; they wanted more clarity on what that data meant for their future.

  2. 2

    Iterative Wireframing

    Low-fidelity prototypes were tested weekly. We focused on the 'Golden Path' of identifying market trends and executing trades with a single gesture.

  3. 3

    Usability Testing

    Validated high-fidelity prototypes with 50+ users. We reduced the task completion time for 'Portfolio Health Check' from 4.5 minutes to 45 seconds.

The Solution

"We didn't just redesign the app; we redesigned the user's relationship with their own financial future through predictive transparency."

The final product introduced a Contextual Command Center. Instead of navigating through deep menus, the UI surface adapts based on time of day, market volatility, and user habits.

  • check_circle Adaptive Glassmorphism: Used depth to prioritize high-risk assets visually.
  • check_circle Natural Language Processing: Replaced complex filter menus with a simple search bar that understands intent.
  • check_circle Proactive Alerts: AI-driven notifications that suggest rebalancing strategies before market shifts occur.

The Impact

+15% Conversion Rate
82% User Retention
-70% Support Tickets
